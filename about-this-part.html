About This Part

This part, also known as the “urbiscript tutorial”, teaches the reader how to program in urbiscript. It goes from the basis to concurrent and event-based programming. No speciﬁc knowledge is expected. There is no need for a C++ compiler, as UObject will not be covered here (see Listing IV). The reference manual contains a terse and complete deﬁnition of the Urbi environment (Section III).
